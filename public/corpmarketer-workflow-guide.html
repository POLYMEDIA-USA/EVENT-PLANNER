Phase 1 — Pre-Event: Lead Capture

1
Sign In & Select Event
Log in with your credentials. On the Dashboard, select the active event you're working. This scopes all your data to that event.
2
Add Leads
Navigate to Leads. Click "Add Lead" and enter: Full Name, Title, Company, Email, Phone, and Alt Email. Each lead is automatically associated with your organization.
3
Continue Adding Leads
Add as many leads as needed. Use the search bar to check for duplicates. Your Supervisor will review and promote leads to the invite list.

Phase 2 — RSVP Period (Managed by Supervisor)

During this phase, your Supervisor promotes your leads to the invite list, sends invitation emails, and monitors RSVPs. You don't need to take action, but you can track status in the Dashboard stats.

📬
Invitees receive an email with Accept and Decline buttons. When they accept, the system generates a QR code and manual check-in code for event day.

Phase 3 — Live Event: QR Check-In

4
Open QR Scanner on Your Phone
Navigate to QR Scanner. Tap "Start Scanner". Allow camera access when prompted.
5
Scan Attendee QR Codes
Point your phone camera at the attendee's QR code. The system instantly shows their name, title, and organization.
6
Manual Check-In (Fallback)
If scanning fails, ask the attendee for their manual check-in code (shown in their confirmation email). Enter it and click "Check In".

What You See After Scanning

After a successful scan, the check-in screen displays:

  • Full name of the attendee
  • Title (e.g., VP Marketing)
  • Company name
  • Organization they belong to

You can immediately add interaction notes — what you discussed, their interests, follow-up actions — then tap "Save Notes".

📝
Log notes while the conversation is fresh! These are visible to your entire team and help with post-event follow-up.

Phase 4 — Live Event: Interaction Notes

7
Navigate to Interactions
Go to My Interactions to see all customers. Select a customer from the list to view their full history.
8
Add Notes for Any Customer
Select a customer, type your notes in the "Add a Note" box, and click "Save Note". Multiple team members can add notes to the same customer.

Phase 1 — Pre-Event: Review & Promote Leads

1
Review Leads from Sales Reps
Navigate to Leads. You see leads from all organizations. Review the list for quality and completeness — ensure contacts have valid emails.
2
Promote Leads to Invited
Go to Invited → Promote Leads tab. Select leads using checkboxes (or "Select All"), then click "Promote to Invited". This moves them into the invitation pipeline.

Phase 2 — Email Campaigns: Invite & Follow Up

3
Send Initial Invitations
Switch to the Invited List tab. Use "Not Yet Invited" to select recipients who haven't been emailed. Choose Invitation type and click "Send Emails".
4
Monitor RSVPs & Send Reminders
Check the RSVP status badges. Use "Pending RSVP" to select non-respondents, choose Reminder type, and send follow-up emails.
5
Send Confirmations with QR Codes
Use "Accepted" to select confirmed attendees. Choose Confirmation type and send. This email includes the QR code and manual check-in code for event day.
⚠️
Duplicate prevention: The system shows email history badges for each invitee and warns you before re-sending the same email type. Always review the send summary panel before clicking "Send Emails".

Phase 3 — Live Event: Monitor Check-Ins & Notes

6
Monitor Real-Time Check-Ins
Navigate to Interactions. As Sales Reps scan QR codes and log notes, you see everything in real-time across all team members. Select any customer to view their full engagement history.
7
Add Supervisor Notes
You can also add your own interaction notes to any customer. Select them from the list, type your notes, and click "Save Note". Your notes are visible to the entire team.
👁️
Full visibility: As a Supervisor, you see notes from ALL sales reps across ALL organizations — giving you a complete picture of team engagement at the event.

Phase 4 — Post-Event: Reports & Analysis

8
Review Overview Reports
Navigate to Reports → Overview tab. View organization-level statistics and a sortable master table of all leads with their statuses, RSVP responses, and interaction counts.
9
Audit Email Log
Switch to the Email Log tab. Search and review every outbound email sent. Click any entry to see a full HTML preview of what the recipient received. Use the print button for records.
