CorpMarketer

Supervisor reference — operational access for team leads

As a Supervisor, you have access to all operational features in CorpMarketer — events, leads, invitations, email campaigns, QR scanning, interactions, and reports. You do not have access to User Management or System Settings (these are Admin-only).

Login URL: https://your-app-url.run.app

Signing In

  1. Navigate to the app URL.
  2. Enter your Email / Username and Password (provided by your Admin).
  3. Click "Sign In".
🔑
Your account is created by an Admin. Contact your Admin if you need your password reset or role changed.

Dashboard

Event Selector

Select an active event to scope your work across pages. Click "Change" to switch events at any time.

Stats Cards

Four cards show platform-wide metrics: Active Events, Total Leads, Invited, and Attended.

Events

◈

View Events

Read-only access — event creation & editing is Admin only

You can view all events with their details — name, date, time, location, status, and description. To create, edit, or delete events, contact your Admin.

📌
Only active events appear in the Dashboard selector and are used for email campaigns.

Leads

◉

Lead Management

Full visibility across all organizations

As a Supervisor, you see leads from all organizations — the same view as an Admin.

  1. Click "Add Lead" to create a new lead.
  2. Fill in: Full Name, Title, Company, Email, Phone, Alt Email.
  3. Click "Create".

Use the search bar to filter by name, email, company, or phone. Click "Edit" or "Delete" to manage existing leads.

Invited & Email Campaigns

✉

Promote Leads & Send Email Campaigns

Two tabs: Promote Leads & Invited List

Promote Leads Tab

Select leads using checkboxes (or "Select All") and click "Promote to Invited" to add them to the invited list.

Invited List Tab

View all invitees with their RSVP status and email history badges.

Sending Emails

  1. Use smart selection buttons to pick recipients: Select All, Pending RSVP, Accepted, Not Yet Invited, or No Reminder Sent.
  2. Choose an email type:
TypeWhen to Use
InvitationInitial event invite with RSVP buttons
ReminderFollow-up for non-respondents
ConfirmationConfirm attendance & send QR code
Event UpdateNotify about changed event details
CustomFree-form with custom subject & body
  1. Review the send summary (shows recipient count and duplicate warnings).
  2. Click "Send Emails".

Email History Badges

Each invitee shows colored badges for emails already sent. This helps you avoid sending duplicate communications:

  • invitation — Initial invite sent
  • reminder — Reminder sent
  • confirmation — Confirmation with QR code sent
  • event_update — Event update sent
  • custom — Custom message sent
⚠️
The system warns you when recipients have already received the selected email type. Always review the send summary before confirming.

QR Scanner

Check in attendees at events using their QR codes.

  1. Click "Start Scanner" to activate your device camera.
  2. Point at the attendee's QR code (from their confirmation email).
  3. The system reads the code and displays the attendee's name, title, and organization.
  4. Optionally add interaction notes and click "Save Notes".

Manual check-in: If the QR code won't scan, enter the attendee's 4-character check-in code (e.g., K7PN — from their confirmation email) and click "Check In".

📱
Works on all modern browsers. Best on mobile devices with rear-facing cameras.

Interactions

A customer-centric note-taking hub with full cross-team visibility (same view as Admin).

  1. Select a customer from the left panel (search by name, title, company, or email).
  2. View their full interaction history — notes, QR check-ins, and RSVP events from all team members.
  3. Add your own notes in the "Add a Note" section and click "Save Note".
👥
Multiple team members can log notes on the same customer. Each note shows who wrote it and when.

Reports

Overview Tab

Organization-level statistics and a sortable master table of all leads with statuses, RSVP responses, and interaction counts.

Email Log Tab

Complete log of all outbound emails. Click any entry to see a PDF-style preview of the full HTML email. Use the print button for records.

Use the search bar to filter by recipient, subject, or email type.

Post-Event Tab

After an event, use the Post-Event tab to review attendees and distribute reports to your sales reps.

  • Attendee list — All checked-in attendees with their interaction counts and which reps engaged them
  • Expandable detail — Click any attendee to see the full interaction history (notes, timestamps, rep names)
  • Rep selection panel — Choose which reps receive a report. Each rep gets only the attendees they interacted with.
  • Email Reports — Send formatted HTML reports to selected reps
  • Download CSV — Export the complete event dataset for your records or further analysis
📊
Tip: Use "Select all reps with interactions" to quickly pick everyone, then adjust as needed before sending.

What Requires an Admin

The following actions require Admin access. Contact your Admin for these:

  • Create, edit, or delete events — Event management is Admin only
  • Create, edit, or delete user accounts — Including changing roles or resetting passwords
  • Configure SMTP email settings — Server host, port, credentials, and from address
  • Update company branding — Company name, logo URL, and app URL

Tips & Best Practices

  • Use smart selection buttons to target the right audience and avoid duplicate emails.
  • Send confirmations to accepted RSVPs — these include the QR code needed for check-in.
  • Check the email log in Reports to verify what was sent and catch any failed deliveries.
  • Select your event first on the Dashboard before working with Leads — it scopes data to that event.
  • Use reminders strategically — send to "Pending RSVP" contacts a few days before the event.
